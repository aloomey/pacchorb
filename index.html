A Little Nonsense Pacman Redesign

This is a fun little modification of the HTML5 Pacman game available at https://github.com/daleharvey/pacman.

Now you get to fly around with a little emojese-chorb, avoid suspiciously ghost-like angels, nom letters, eat suspiciously shaped pills and rolyroly down those in your way to complete your tasks and finally start on your actual secret mission.

Missed what it was all about? Find out all about Shoo and chorbs and everything nice here: ChorbSoo !
